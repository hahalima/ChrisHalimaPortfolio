Streak

Android and Pebble application that incentives users to consistently be healthy by rewarding them with money from users who break their health streak. It tracks the user’s health progress by recognizing and recording whether user is near a designated gym or going to eat out in a healthy or unhealthy location with a phone and Pebble.
